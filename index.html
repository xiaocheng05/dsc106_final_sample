Key Takeaways

🌫️

Aerosol concentration is spatially uneven, and its relationship with temperature and precipitation changes by country. The visualization helps turn a large climate model dataset into an explorable regional story.

🌍
A globe makes regional aerosol differences visible.
📈
Country dashboards reveal different climate relationships.
🌧️
Precipitation is noisy, so it should be read as a pattern to investigate.

From global signal to local impact

The same global aerosol trend can appear very differently across countries, showing why climate stories need both worldwide context and regional detail.
